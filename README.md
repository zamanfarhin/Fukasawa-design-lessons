# Without Thought — A study of Naoto Fukasawa

An interactive design study exploring the philosophy of Naoto Fukasawa — Without Thought, Super Normal, and design dissolving in behavior — through nine of his notable objects, and an original concept designed in his language.

## What's here

- **The collection** — nine Fukasawa objects rendered as an interactive catalog grid, each one waking up on click (the CD player spins, the lamp glows, the kettle steams). A master light switch turns the whole room on at once.
- **Learn about him** — a short biography.
- **Understand his philosophy** — Without Thought, design dissolving in behavior, and Super Normal, in his own language.
- **Inspired bracelet** — an original wellness object concept, "the amulet band," designed from the ground up using Fukasawa's own principles. Includes a mind map of every design decision, a hand-drawn widget language for the raised interaction state, and the original sketches the concept was built from.

## Why

Most Fukasawa-inspired case studies stop at admiration. This one tries to think the way he thinks — starting from behavior, not features — and shows the actual decision trail for an original object, not just a mood board.

## Built with

Static HTML/CSS/JS, single file, no dependencies beyond Google Fonts. Sketches are embedded directly as base64 so the page never breaks on move or download.
